# Investment Research & Quant Strategies Repository

Welcome! This repository is designed to **guide market participants, students, and professionals to a solid starting point in equity and quantitative analysis**. Rather than the scattered, generic material you find online, here you’ll find a rigorously organized knowledge base, structured to accelerate your growth as a market player and highlight the expertise and practical experience informing these resources.

***

## Repository Structure

### 1. Fundamental Analysis (`Fundamental_Analysis/`)
Organized by folder: `Ticker_Name_Exchange/`
- **Step By Step Process (1):** Detailed workflow for company analysis.
- **FCFF Spreadsheet (2):** Excel templates for fundamental DCF modeling.
- **Colab Notebook (3):** Python notebooks for reproducible financial analysis.
- **Report (4):** Professional equity research reports.

### 2. Quantitative Strategies (`Quant/`)
Each strategy has its own folder:
- **L&S Arbitrage Strategy (1):** Execution-ready arbitrage models.
- **Mean Reversal Strategy (2):** Systematic mean reversion templates.
- **Trend Strategy (3):** Models capturing persistent market trends.
- **Market Neutral Strategy (4):** Examples of hedged, market-neutral approaches.

#### Examples of Live Quant Algorithms in Use
Current working strategies include:
- **Peak Volume Strategy**
- **Day Trading Portfolio Strategy**
- **Swing Trade Portfolio Strategy**
- **Earnings & Dividend Events Strategy**
- *(In Progress)* Option x Asset Strategy

***


## Mission

This repository aims to:
- **Provide a clear learning path** for solid investment analysis — not widely available or organized elsewhere.
- **Showcase advanced quant strategies** alongside practical fundamental analysis modules.
- **Connect experienced and aspiring market participants** through transparent, actionable research.
